# Scene 1 Welcome and Demo Orientation

## Introduction

**Welcome** gives the seller a simple way to open the clinical-supply story. The page explains the regulated supply and quality journey before the demo moves into dashboards, signals, graph relationships, cold-chain coverage, orders, predictive analytics, natural-language SQL, and governed agents.

Life sciences customers often struggle to explain how a quality signal becomes an operational decision. A regulatory bulletin, GxP deviation, cold-chain advisory, manufacturer note, or trial-site supply risk may start in one system while product, order, depot, and audit evidence live somewhere else.

Oracle AI Database helps address that challenge by bringing those data types and AI workflows onto the same governed data foundation. In this scene, the welcome page makes the demo easy to tell as one connected clinical-supply risk story rather than disconnected technology features.

Estimated Time: 5 minutes

### Objectives

In this scene, you will learn how to introduce the demo, what clinical-supply story the pages follow, and how to move into the guided flow.

## Task 1: Review the clinical supply story

![Welcome page with the eight-use-case clinical supply story highlighted](images/control-tower.png)

Use the welcome page to frame the demo for clinical operations, quality, supply chain, regulatory, and manufacturing leaders.

1. Click **Welcome** in the sidebar.
2. Review **Eight use cases, one clinical supply risk story** in the top panel.
3. Read the eight numbered use case summaries. The point is to make the demo easy to tell as one regulated supply journey: quality teams identify a signal, operations teams trace exposure, cold-chain teams protect controlled inventory, analytics forecast risk, and agents recommend governed actions.
4. Review the visible **Key Life Sciences Use Cases Featured** tiles.

The seller talk track should stay business focused. Lead with clinical supply continuity, quality traceability, regulatory readiness, and governed action.

## Task 2: Continue the demo

![Start the demo button highlighted on the welcome page](images/control-tower.png)

1. Click **Start the demo**.
2. Confirm the demo moves to the next page.
