# Scene 2 Data Foundation

## Introduction

**Data Foundation** prepares and verifies the trusted Seer Lifesciences dataset used throughout the clinical-supply journey. It shows that regulated products, quality signals, clinical supply orders, product vectors, signal vectors, and semantic matches are ready so every later scene starts from the same governed baseline.

Life sciences data is often fragmented across clinical operations, quality management, regulatory systems, manufacturing operations, depots, and logistics partners. That fragmentation makes it harder to answer one practical question with confidence: *Which products, sites, orders, and quality signals are connected right now?*

**Oracle AI Database** helps address that challenge by keeping transactional, JSON, graph, spatial, vector, machine learning, and agent audit data close to the same governed schema. In this scene, the dataset preparation view gives the seller a quick proof point that the demo is running against connected live data rather than disconnected sample screens.

## Task 1: Prepare the dataset

![Data Foundation page with the Restore Demo Data button and record counts highlighted](images/data-foundation.png)

Perform the following set of steps to confirm that the data footprint is ready before presenting the rest of the demo.

1. Click **Data Foundation** in the sidebar.
2. In **Prepare the Dataset**, review the live record counts.
3. If the dataset needs to be reset, click **Restore Demo Data**.
4. Wait for the progress indicator to finish and review the refreshed counts.

In the current demo dataset, the page shows **14,622** tracked records, including **79** regulated products, **5,000** quality signals, **3,000** clinical supply orders, **79** product vectors, **5,000** signal vectors, and **1,464** semantic matches.

**Note:** Sample values may change after data refreshes or rebuilds. Verify live output before presenting, then explain the business takeaway.

## Task 2: Review what gets loaded

![Data Foundation page with What Gets Loaded carousel highlighted](images/data-foundation.png)
