# Scene 6 Rights, Capacity, & Live Event Coverage

## Introduction

**Rights, Capacity, & Live Event Coverage** helps teams decide where audience demand, rights availability, partner activations, and coverage capacity intersect.

Media teams struggle when rights, coverage, activation, and audience demand data live in separate systems. That separation makes it difficult to explain why a coverage or rights decision should be made now.

**Oracle AI Database** helps address these challenges by keeping spatial geometry and operating records together. In this scene, **Oracle Spatial** helps teams connect audience demand, coverage readiness, partner activations, and rights availability in one operating view.

Estimated Time: **10 minutes**

In this scene, you will learn what geographic or rights decision the page supports, what evidence the user should inspect, and what action the business may take next.
